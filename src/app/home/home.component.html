Project Overview

This web-based tool allows players to edit their Bloodborne save files directly from their browser. Customize your character, inventory, and more, without the need for third-party software!

Features

  • Edit character stats, echos, name, appearance.
  • Add or remove items or weapons from inventory.
  • Unlock all items at maximum quantities.
  • No data stored on our servers â€“ privacy is a priority.

How It Works

  1. Upload your Bloodborne save file.
  2. Navigate through the editor to change stats, items, or settings.
  3. Save and download the modified file.
  4. Load it back into your game to see the changes.
